Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a trip?

Simply log in to your account, go to the “Booking” section, select your preferred destination, travel date, and confirm your booking.

2. Which destinations are currently available?

We currently offer one-day trips to Mysore, Ooty, Shimoga, and Goa.

3. How does the one-day trip work?

You arrive at our office by 5:00 AM on the travel date, board the designated bus, enjoy sightseeing at your destination, and return the same night or early next morning.

4. Do you provide hotel stays?

No, our current packages are designed for one-day trips without overnight stays.

5. How do I make the payment?

After selecting your trip, you’ll be redirected to the payment page. You can complete the payment online using our secure payment process.

6. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking through the 'Trips' section in your dashboard; however, refunds are not available.

7. Can I customize my itinerary?

Yes. Our default itinerary is provided for each trip, but you can edit and add your own activities in the “Itinerary” section between 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM.
